African Dream

AFRICANDREAM was established in 2000 to make a difference in the economy of Limpopo. AFRICANDREAM is a black owned entity, owned entirely by
a woman. The main purpose of the company is event management with an intense focus on catering and equipment hire. The company targets corporate
the government, parastatals, individuals as well as NGOs.

The company's mission is to serve it's clients with pride and become the leading player in providing management and conference solutions to the clients.
